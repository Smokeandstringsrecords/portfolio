About Shivam

Shivam Madaan is a 24-year-old Guitarist and a Music Producer from Delhi, India, known for his Fingerstyle playing and Indie & Pop Releases.

His musical journey began in school choirs and late-night jam sessions, eventually leading him from his first guitar to writing his first original songs. He made his professional debut in 2022 and has since released multiple tracks, collaborating with other artists and steadily building a loyal audience across streaming and social platforms.

Music Career

Shivam draws inspiration from a wide range of artists across pop, indie, folk, and classical music. Her arrangements often pair acoustic guitar or piano with subtle electronic textures, allowing the voice and story to remain front and center.
